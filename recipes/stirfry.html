Barker's Veggie Stir-Fry With Egg

This is a delicious recipe that doesn't take much time to make. I've been making it for years. Feel free to tweak the recipe to your liking. I hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • oil
  • eggs
  • broccoli
  • cauliflower
  • carrots
  • cabbage
  • onion
  • garlic
  • peppers
  • soy sauce
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut up vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Heat up wok and add a little bit of oil.
  3. Once oil is hot, add aromatics and cook while stirring for a couple of mintues.
  4. Add hardest veggies first (carrots, broccoli, etc...) and cook these for a few minutes.
  5. Add softer vegetables (zuchinni, cabbage, etc...) and cook these for a bit.
  6. Add whatever spices/salt to taste.
  7. Start frying 2 eggs in oil in a seperate pan.
  8. Add soy sauce to veggies if desired.
  9. Plate and Enjoy!
